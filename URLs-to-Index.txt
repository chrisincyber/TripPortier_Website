HOW TO SUBMIT:
==============

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Select your property: tripportier.com
3. Click the search bar at top
4. Paste each URL (copy from list above)
5. Press Enter
6. Click "Request Indexing"
7. Wait for confirmation
8. Move to next URL

TIPS:
-----
- Start with Priority 1 URLs (just fixed)
- Google limits requests, so spread them out if needed
- Check back in 24-48 hours to see indexing status
- You can check status anytime by inspecting the URL again
