Flight Tracking

Add your flight number and date, and we'll track it using real-time data from FlightAware. You'll get updates on:

  • Departure and arrival times
  • Gate changes
  • Delays and cancellations
  • Terminal and baggage claim info

TripPortier sends smart notifications including:

  • Day-of-flight reminder: Morning summary of your flight
  • Leave for airport: Based on estimated travel time
  • Gate changes: When your gate is updated
  • Delays: If departure time changes significantly
  • Boarding time: When it's time to head to the gate

We use FlightAware, a leading flight tracking service, for real-time data. While highly accurate, always verify critical information with your airline. Flight data is updated every 20 minutes when your flight is active.

eSIM Plans

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan without a physical SIM card. It's built into newer smartphones and lets you add a data plan instantly - perfect for international travel.

Most recent iPhones support eSIM, including:

  • iPhone XS and newer
  • iPhone SE (2nd generation and newer)

Your phone must be carrier-unlocked for eSIM to work with international data plans. Check with your carrier if you're unsure.

After purchase, your eSIM is delivered instantly in the app:

  1. Go to "My eSIMs" in the app
  2. Tap your new eSIM
  3. Follow the installation instructions
  4. Once installed, activate it when you arrive at your destination

We recommend installing before you travel (while on WiFi) so it's ready when you land.

We offer data plans for 200+ countries and regions worldwide:

  • Local plans: Single-country coverage
  • Regional plans: Cover multiple countries (Europe, Asia, etc.)
  • Global plans: Worldwide coverage

Troubleshooting

Check these settings:

  1. Open iPhone Settings > TripPortier > Notifications
  2. Ensure notifications are enabled
  3. Check that "Allow Notifications" is on
  4. Make sure your flight is added correctly with the right date

Also ensure Background App Refresh is enabled for TripPortier.

Try these steps:

  1. Ensure your phone is carrier-unlocked
  2. Check that the eSIM is set as the data line in Settings > Cellular
  3. Enable Data Roaming for the eSIM line
  4. Restart your phone
  5. Make sure you're in a covered area
