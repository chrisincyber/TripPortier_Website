11. AI Email Import Service

11.1 How It Works

TripPortier provides an AI-powered email forwarding service that allows you to automatically create itinerary items from booking confirmation emails:

  • You receive a unique email address (e.g., yourname@import.tripportier.com)
  • Forward booking confirmation emails (flights, hotels, restaurants, activities) to this address
  • AI analyzes the email content and attachments to extract booking details
  • Extracted bookings appear in your app for review and approval before being added to your trips

11.2 Email Processing

By using the Email Import feature, you consent to the following:

  • Emails you forward are received and processed by our secure email service (Amazon SES)
  • Email content and attachments (PDFs, images) are analyzed by AI (Google Gemini) to extract booking information
  • Extracted data includes: booking type, confirmation numbers, dates, times, locations, prices, and provider details
  • PDF attachments from emails are stored securely in Firebase Storage for attachment to your itinerary items
  • We retain pending booking data for up to 30 days; after processing, only the extracted itinerary item is stored
  • Original emails are not permanently stored after processing

11.3 Data Accuracy

AI extraction may not be 100% accurate:

  • Always review extracted booking details before accepting them into your trip
  • You can edit any extracted fields (title, dates, times, locations) before confirmation
  • We display a confidence indicator to help you gauge extraction reliability
  • We are not responsible for errors in AI-extracted booking information

11.4 Acceptable Use

When using Email Import, you agree:

  • Only forward emails that you have the right to share (your own booking confirmations)
  • Do not forward emails containing sensitive personal information beyond booking details
  • Do not use the service to process spam, malicious content, or unauthorized communications
  • Your unique email address is personal and should not be shared publicly
